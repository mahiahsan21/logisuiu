About Us

Welcome to Logis, where we are dedicated to simplifying the process of lost item recovery. Our mission is to provide a reliable platform for individuals to report lost items and for owners to reclaim their belongings swiftly. Guided by principles of honesty and efficiency, we offer advanced features such as intuitive search options and secure data management, ensuring a seamless experience for our users. At Logis, we prioritize the privacy and security of our users information, and our committed support team is always available to assist with any inquiries or concerns. Our Services

Our Services

Search for Lost Items

Allow users to search the database for lost items based on various criteria such as keywords, categories, date range, and location. Provide advanced search options to facilitate easier retrieval.

Claim a Found Item

Enable users who believe they have found a lost item to report it through the system. Include fields for describing the found item, location where it was found, and contact information for the finder.

Update Item Status

Allow users to update the status of their lost or found items. This could include marking an item as claimed, returned, or still missing. Ensure that only authorized users can make these updates to maintain data integrity.

Trucking

Provide real-time tracking of the delivery process within the system. Users should be able to monitor the status of their delivery, including when the item is picked up, in transit, and delivered. Integration with tracking APIs from logistics partners can facilitate this functionality.

Packaging

Secure packaging option,generate shipping labels directly from the system, pre-filled with the recipient's address information. Integrate with shipping carriers to provide real-time tracking information so both the finder and owner can monitor the package's journey.

Report a lost item

Provide a form where users can input details about the item they have lost, including a description, location where it was last seen, date of loss, and contact information.

Notifications and Alerts

Implement a notification system that alerts users when a found item matches the description of their lost item. Users should be able to opt-in to receive these notifications via email or SMS.

  • Implement a notification system that automatically alerts users when a found item matches the description of a lost item they reported.
  • Notify users about updates to the status of their reported lost or found items.
  • Send reminder alerts to users who have reported lost items but have not taken any further action for a specified period.

Feedback and Suggestions

Allow users to provide feedback and suggestions for improving the services section. This could include a feedback form or a dedicated section where users can share their ideas for enhancing the user experience.

Privacy Considerations

Provide users with options to control the visibility of their posts and adhere to Facebook's privacy policies and guidelines.

  • Ensure that users' privacy is respected and that sensitive information is not exposed through Facebook posts
  • Implement appropriate privacy settings and obtain consent from users before posting their lost or found item details on Facebook.
